About

Who am I

I am sure you just want to check my work out, so I'll be quick. I’m a concert pianist turned web developer. I began learning web development one year ago. I started off with learning the basics of HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Ruby, C++ then I decided to focus on learning Javascript and front end frameworks.

I have always had a passion for classical music and a love of technology. At a young age, I chose to focus on establishing a career as pianist and film music composer. At the same time I continued to satisfy my tech interests. I believe that programming is an art form and that there is a great parallel between music composition and web development. That is the ability to create and shape something out of nothing.

Outside of my digital life I have a number of other interests as well. I love watching and playing tennis, hiking and enjoying nature, and spending time with my beautiful wife and good friends.



What I do

I’m a passionate javascript programmer who specializes in front-end development. I have a powerful team spirit, a creative mind and a great work ethic. I believe in pretty designs and clean coding, and take pride in writing the kind of code that others can read and collaborate on.

I enjoy working with Photoshop and convert designs to high-performance code whilst respecting the designer’s compositions. I Use modern techniques to build websites and apps that look great on the browser or on the phone. The mobile compatibility is one of my main concerns so I’m always focused on making my applications work nice and smoothly on mobile devices.

I’m constantly learning new technologies and thriving to improve my skills. I spend most of time reading books, attending workshops, and blogging about my experience.
